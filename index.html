从零开始读Harry Potter

Posted on 2021-01-15

从零开始读Harry Potter的第一章，下面应该会对每部书中出现的人物、剧情、英文语法等做出记录。

THE BOY WHO LIVED

《Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone》第一章
出场人物

  • Mr. & Mrs. Dursley
    • live in number four, Privet Drive,
    • director of a firm called Grunnings,which made drills
  • Dudley
    • son of Mr. & Mrs. Dursley
  • tabby cat

    故事情节

有意思的句子

Mrs. Dursley was thin and blonde and had nearly twice the usual amount of neck, which came in very useful as she spent so much of her time craning over garden fences, spying on the neighbors.

这段话很有意思，说Dursley太太金发碧眼，脖子比较长，“which came in very useful as…”，里面含着Rowling对这个人物的嘲讽。

有意思的词汇

Mrs. Dursley gossiped away happily as she wrestled a screaming Dudley into his high chair.

wrestle本意是“摔跤、扭打”，这里用来说明安抚吵闹的孩子的动作

None of them noticed a large, tawny owl flutter past the window.

flutter,用于表示鸟儿震动翅膀，有点在外面乱飞的意思

另：

  • briefcase 公文包
  • cloak 斗篷、巫师袍
  • getups 装扮
  • steering wheel 方向盘
  • weirdo 奇怪的人
  • The nerve of him！ 不嫌丢人！
